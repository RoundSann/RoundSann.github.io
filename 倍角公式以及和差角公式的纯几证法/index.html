以一种比较繁琐的几何方式暴力证明了倍角公式以及和差角公式

倍角公式

如图,在Rt△ACBRt\triangle ACBRt△ACB中,ADADAD平分∠BAC\angle BAC∠BAC交BCBCBC于点DDD,DE⊥ABDE\perp ABDE⊥AB交ABABAB于点EEE
我们设∠CAD=∠DAE=α\angle CAD=\angle DAE=\alpha∠CAD=∠DAE=α,试求2α2\alpha2α的各三角函数值

我们不妨设AB=1AB=1AB=1,则AC=AE=cos⁡(2α),BC=sin⁡(2α)AC=AE=\cos(2\alpha),BC=\sin(2\alpha)AC=AE=cos(2α),BC=sin(2α)
从而DC=DE=tan⁡(α)cos⁡(2α)DC=DE=\tan(\alpha)\cos(2\alpha)DC=DE=tan(α)cos(2α)
进而,我们有BE=1−cos⁡(2α)BE=1-\cos(2\alpha)BE=1−cos(2α),BD=sin⁡(2α)−cos⁡(2α)tan⁡(α)BD=\sin(2\alpha)-\cos(2\alpha)\tan(\alpha)BD=sin(2α)−cos(2α)tan(α)
同时,我们有△BDE∽△BAC\triangle BDE\backsim\triangle BAC△BDE∽△BAC
也就是有:

BDBA=BEBC=DEACsin⁡(2α)−tan⁡(α)cos⁡(2α)=1−cos⁡(2α)sin⁡(2α)=tan⁡(α)cos⁡(2α)cos⁡(2α)\begin{align*} \frac{BD}{BA}&=\frac{BE}{BC}=\frac{DE}{AC}\\ \sin(2\alpha)-\tan(\alpha)\cos(2\alpha)&=\frac{1-\cos(2\alpha)}{\sin(2\alpha)}=\frac{\tan(\alpha)\cos(2\alpha)}{\cos(2\alpha)} \end{align*}BABD​sin(2α)−tan(α)cos(2α)​=BCBE​=ACDE​=sin(2α)1−cos(2α)​=cos(2α)tan(α)cos(2α)​​

从而我们就会有:

{1−cos⁡(2α)=tan⁡(α)sin⁡(2α)sin⁡(2α)−tan⁡(α)cos⁡(2α)=tan⁡(α)\begin{cases} 1-\cos(2\alpha)=\tan(\alpha)\sin(2\alpha)\\ \sin(2\alpha)-\tan(\alpha)\cos(2\alpha)=\tan(\alpha) \end{cases}{1−cos(2α)=tan(α)sin(2α)sin(2α)−tan(α)cos(2α)=tan(α)​

也就是:

{tan⁡2(α)sin⁡(2α)+tan⁡(α)cos⁡(2α)=tan⁡(α)sin⁡(2α)−tan⁡(α)cos⁡(2α)=tan⁡(α)\begin{cases} \tan^2(\alpha)\sin(2\alpha)+\tan(\alpha)\cos(2\alpha)=\tan(\alpha)\\ \sin(2\alpha)-\tan(\alpha)\cos(2\alpha)=\tan(\alpha) \end{cases}{tan2(α)sin(2α)+tan(α)cos(2α)=tan(α)sin(2α)−tan(α)cos(2α)=tan(α)​

从而就有:

tan⁡2(α)sin⁡(2α)+sin⁡(2α)=2tan⁡(α)\tan^2(\alpha)\sin(2\alpha)+\sin(2\alpha)=2\tan(\alpha)tan2(α)sin(2α)+sin(2α)=2tan(α)

也就可以推出:

sin⁡(2α)=2tan⁡(α)1+tan⁡2(α)cos⁡(2α)=tan⁡2(α)−1tan⁡2(α)+1tan⁡(2α)=sin⁡(2α)cos⁡(2α)=2tan⁡(α)tan⁡2(x)−1\begin{align*} \sin(2\alpha)&=\frac{2\tan(\alpha)}{1+\tan^2(\alpha)}\\ \cos(2\alpha)&=\frac{\tan^2(\alpha)-1}{\tan^2(\alpha)+1}\\ \tan(2\alpha)&=\frac{\sin(2\alpha)}{\cos(2\alpha)}=\frac{2\tan(\alpha)}{\tan^2(x)-1} \end{align*}sin(2α)cos(2α)tan(2α)​=1+tan2(α)2tan(α)​=tan2(α)+1tan2(α)−1​=cos(2α)sin(2α)​=tan2(x)−12tan(α)​​

综上,也就有:

{sin⁡(2α)=2sin⁡(α)cos⁡(α)cos⁡(2α)=cos⁡2(α)−sin⁡2(α)tan⁡(2α)=2tan⁡(α)tan⁡2(α)−1\begin{cases} \sin(2\alpha)&=2\sin(\alpha)\cos(\alpha)\\ \cos(2\alpha)&=\cos^2(\alpha)-\sin^2(\alpha)\\ \tan(2\alpha)&=\dfrac{2\tan(\alpha)}{\tan^2(\alpha)-1} \end{cases}⎩⎨⎧​sin(2α)cos(2α)tan(2α)​=2sin(α)cos(α)=cos2(α)−sin2(α)=tan2(α)−12tan(α)​​

和差角公式

如图,设BD=rBD=rBD=r,BC=aBC=aBC=a,AC=bAC=bAC=b,AB=cAB=cAB=c,∠CBD=α\angle CBD=\alpha∠CBD=α,∠ABD=β\angle ABD=\beta∠ABD=β
则CD=rsin⁡(α),BC=a=rcos⁡(α),DE=rsin⁡(β),BE=rcos⁡(β)CD=r\sin(\alpha),BC=a=r\cos(\alpha),DE=r\sin(\beta),BE=r\cos(\beta)CD=rsin(α),BC=a=rcos(α),DE=rsin(β),BE=rcos(β)
而AE=c−BE=c−rcos⁡(β)AE=c-BE=c-r\cos(\beta)AE=c−BE=c−rcos(β),AD=b−CD=b−rsin⁡(α)AD=b-CD=b-r\sin(\alpha)AD=b−CD=b−rsin(α)
且有△ADE∽△ABC\triangle ADE\backsim\triangle ABC△ADE∽△ABC,也就是有:

ADAB=AEAC=DEBCb−rsin⁡(α)c=c−rcos⁡(β)b=rsin⁡(β)rcos⁡(α)\begin{gather*} \frac{AD}{AB}=\frac{AE}{AC}=\frac{DE}{BC}\\ \frac{b-r\sin(\alpha)}{c}=\frac{c-r\cos(\beta)}{b}=\frac{r\sin(\beta)}{r\cos(\alpha)} \end{gather*}ABAD​=ACAE​=BCDE​cb−rsin(α)​=bc−rcos(β)​=rcos(α)rsin(β)​​

那么也就有

{ccos⁡(α)−rcos⁡(α)cos⁡(β)=bsin⁡(β)bcos⁡(α)−rsin⁡(α)cos⁡(α)=csin⁡(β)\begin{cases} c\cos(\alpha)-r\cos(\alpha)\cos(\beta)=b\sin(\beta)\\ b\cos(\alpha)-r\sin(\alpha)\cos(\alpha)=c\sin(\beta) \end{cases}{ccos(α)−rcos(α)cos(β)=bsin(β)bcos(α)−rsin(α)cos(α)=csin(β)​

我们不妨令sin⁡(α+β)=x=bc\sin(\alpha+\beta)=x=\dfrac{b}{c}sin(α+β)=x=cb​,那么就有b=csin⁡(α+β)b=c\sin(\alpha+\beta)b=csin(α+β),带入即可得到:

{ccos⁡(α)−rcos⁡(α)cos⁡(β)=csin⁡(α+β)sin⁡(β)csin⁡(α+β)cos⁡(α)−rsin⁡(α)cos⁡(α)=csin⁡(β)\begin{cases} c\cos(\alpha)-r\cos(\alpha)\cos(\beta)=c\sin(\alpha+\beta)\sin(\beta)\\ c\sin(\alpha+\beta)\cos(\alpha)-r\sin(\alpha)\cos(\alpha)=c\sin(\beta) \end{cases}{ccos(α)−rcos(α)cos(β)=csin(α+β)sin(β)csin(α+β)cos(α)−rsin(α)cos(α)=csin(β)​

也就有:

{c(cos⁡(α)−sin⁡(α+β)sin⁡(β))=rcos⁡(α)cos⁡(β)c(sin⁡(α+β)cos⁡(α)−sin⁡(β))=rsin⁡(α)cos⁡(α)\begin{cases} c(\cos(\alpha)-\sin(\alpha+\beta)\sin(\beta))=r\cos(\alpha)\cos(\beta)\\ c(\sin(\alpha+\beta)\cos(\alpha)-\sin(\beta))=r\sin(\alpha)\cos(\alpha) \end{cases}{c(cos(α)−sin(α+β)sin(β))=rcos(α)cos(β)c(sin(α+β)cos(α)−sin(β))=rsin(α)cos(α)​

两者求比:

cos⁡(α)−sin⁡(α+β)sin⁡(β)sin⁡(α+β)cos⁡(α)−sin⁡(β)=cos⁡(β)sin⁡(α)sin⁡(α)cos⁡(α)−sin⁡(α+β)sin⁡(α)sin⁡(β)=sin⁡(α+β)cos⁡(α)cos⁡(β)−sin⁡(β)cos⁡(β)sin⁡(α+β)(sin⁡(α)sin⁡(β)+cos⁡(α)cos⁡(β))=sin⁡(α)cos⁡(α)+sin⁡(β)cos⁡(β)\begin{gather*} \frac{\cos(\alpha)-\sin(\alpha+\beta)\sin(\beta)}{\sin(\alpha+\beta)\cos(\alpha)-\sin(\beta)}=\frac{\cos(\beta)}{\sin(\alpha)}\\ \sin(\alpha)\cos(\alpha)-\sin(\alpha+\beta)\sin(\alpha)\sin(\beta)=\sin(\alpha+\beta)\cos(\alpha)\cos(\beta)-\sin(\beta)\cos(\beta)\\ \sin(\alpha+\beta)(\sin(\alpha)\sin(\beta)+\cos(\alpha)\cos(\beta))=\sin(\alpha)\cos(\alpha)+\sin(\beta)\cos(\beta)\\ \end{gather*}sin(α+β)cos(α)−sin(β)cos(α)−sin(α+β)sin(β)​=sin(α)cos(β)​sin(α)cos(α)−sin(α+β)sin(α)sin(β)=sin(α+β)cos(α)cos(β)−sin(β)cos(β)sin(α+β)(sin(α)sin(β)+cos(α)cos(β))=sin(α)cos(α)+sin(β)cos(β)​
sin⁡(α+β)=sin⁡(α)cos⁡(α)+sin⁡(β)cos⁡(β)sin⁡(α)sin⁡(β)+cos⁡(α)cos⁡(β)=sin⁡(α)cos⁡(α)(sin⁡2(β)+cos⁡2(β))+sin⁡(β)cos⁡(β)(sin⁡2(α)+cos⁡2(α))sin⁡(α)sin⁡(β)+cos⁡(α)cos⁡(β)=sin⁡(α)cos⁡(β)+cos⁡(α)sin⁡(β)\begin{align*} \sin(\alpha+\beta)&=\frac{\sin(\alpha)\cos(\alpha)+\sin(\beta)\cos(\beta)}{\sin(\alpha)\sin(\beta)+\cos(\alpha)\cos(\beta)}\\ &=\frac{\sin(\alpha)\cos(\alpha)(\sin^2(\beta)+\cos^2(\beta))+\sin(\beta)\cos(\beta)(\sin^2(\alpha)+\cos^2(\alpha))}{\sin(\alpha)\sin(\beta)+\cos(\alpha)\cos(\beta)}\\ &=\sin(\alpha)\cos(\beta)+\cos(\alpha)\sin(\beta) \end{align*}sin(α+β)​=sin(α)sin(β)+cos(α)cos(β)sin(α)cos(α)+sin(β)cos(β)​=sin(α)sin(β)+cos(α)cos(β)sin(α)cos(α)(sin2(β)+cos2(β))+sin(β)cos(β)(sin2(α)+cos2(α))​=sin(α)cos(β)+cos(α)sin(β)​
